Abstract#==#Word Representations such as word embeddings have been shown to significantly
improve (semi-)supervised NER for the English language. In this work we
investigate whether word representations can also boost (semi-)supervised NER
in Spanish. To do so, we use word representations as additional features in a
linear chain Conditional Random Field (CRF) classifier. 
Experimental results (82.44 F-score on the CoNLL-2002 corpus) show that our
approach is comparable to some state-of-the-art Deep Learning approaches for
Spanish, in particular when using cross-lingual Word Representations.
