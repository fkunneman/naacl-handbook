Abstract#==#This paper introduces a Japanese Named Entity (NE) corpus of various genres. We
annotated 136 documents in the Balanced Corpus of Contemporary Written Japanese
with the eight types of NE tags defined by Information Retrieval and Extraction
Exercise. The NE corpus consists of six types of genres of documents such as
blogs, magazines, white papers, and so on, and the corpus contains 2,464 NE
tags in
total.
