Abstract#==#This paper describes Scorpio, the ILLC-UvA Adaptation System
submitted to the IT-DOMAIN translation task at WMT 2016, which participated
with
the language pair of English-Dutch. This system consolidates the ideas
in our previous work on latent variable models for adaptation, and
demonstrates their effectiveness in a competitive setting.
