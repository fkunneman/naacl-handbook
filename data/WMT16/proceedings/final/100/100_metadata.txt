Abstract#==#This paper describes the TALP–UPC system in the Spanish–English WMT 2016
biomedical shared task. Our system is a standard phrase-based system enhanced
with vocabulary expansion using bilingual word embeddings and a characterbased
neural language model with rescoring. The former focuses on resolving
out-of-vocabulary words, while the latter enhances the fluency of the system.
The two modules progressively improve the final translation as measured by a
combination of several lexical metrics.
