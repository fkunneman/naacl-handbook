Abstract#==#This paper describes the participation of Prompsit Language Engineering and the
Universitat d’Alacant in the shared task on document alignment at the First
Conference on Machine Translation (WMT 2016). Two systems have been submitted,
corresponding to two different versions of the tool Bitextor: the last stable
release, version 4.1, and the newest one, version 5.0. The paper describes the
main features of each version of the tool and discusses the results obtained on
the datasets published for the shared task.
