Abstract#==#This article describes the Aalto University entry to the English-to-Finnish
news translation shared task in WMT 2016.
Our segmentation method combines the strengths of rule-based and unsupervised
morphology.
We also attempt to correct errors in the boundary markings by post-processing
with a neural morph boundary predictor.
