Abstract#==#This paper describes the AFRL-MITLL statistical machine translation systems and
the improvements that were developed during the WMT16 evaluation campaign. New
techniques applied this year include Neural Machine Translation, a unique
selection process for language modelling data, additional out-of-vocabulary
transliteration techniques, and morphology generation.
