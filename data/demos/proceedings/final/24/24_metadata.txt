Abstract#==#We present TranscRater, an open-source tool for  automatic speech recognition
(ASR) quality estimation (QE). The tool allows users to perform ASR evaluation
bypassing the need of reference transcripts and confidence information, which
is common to current assessment protocols. TranscRater includes: i) methods to
extract a variety of quality indicators from (signal, transcription) pairs and
ii) machine  learning algorithms which make possible to build ASR QE models
exploiting the extracted features. Confirming the positive results of previous
evaluations, new experiments with TranscRater indicate its effectiveness both
in WER prediction and transcription ranking tasks.
