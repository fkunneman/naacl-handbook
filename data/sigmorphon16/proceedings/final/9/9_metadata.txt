Abstract#==#Syllabification is sometimes influenced by morphological boundaries. We show
that incorporating morphological information can improve the accuracy of
orthographic syllabification in English and German. Surprisingly, unsupervised
segmenters, such as Morfessor, can be more useful for this purpose than the
supervised ones.
