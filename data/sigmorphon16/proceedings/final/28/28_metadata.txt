Abstract#==#We describe our approach and experiments in the context of the SIGMORPHON
2016 Shared Task on Morphological Reinflection. The results show that the
methods of Nicolai et al. (2015) perform well on typologically diverse
languages. We also discuss language-specific heuristics and errors.
