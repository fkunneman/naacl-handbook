Abstract#==#This paper proposes a new task in argument mining in online debates. The task
includes three annotations steps that result in fine-grained annotations of
agreement and disagreement at a propositional level. We report on the results
of a pilot annotation task on identifying sentences that are directly addressed
in the comment.
