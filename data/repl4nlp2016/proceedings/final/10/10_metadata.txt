Abstract#==#This paper introduces a parameterization for word embeddings produced by the
Random Indexing framework. The parameterization introduces position specific
weights in the context windows, and the approach is shown to improve the
performance in both word similarity and sentiment classification tasks. We also
demonstrate the relation between Random Indexing and Convolutional Neural
Networks.
