Abstract#==#This paper presents the SeeDev Task of the BioNLP Shared Task 2016. The purpose
of the SeeDev Task is the extraction from scientific articles of the
descriptions of genetic and molecular mechanisms involved in seed development
of the model plant, Arabidopsis thaliana. The SeeDev task consists in the
extraction of many different events involving a wide range of entity types
accurately reflecting the complexity of the biological mechanisms. The corpus
is composed of paragraphs selected from the full-texts of relevant scientific
papers. In this paper, we describe the organization of the SeeDev task, the
corpus characteristics, and the metrics used for the evaluation of participant
systems. We analyze and discuss the final results of the seven participant
systems to the test. The best F-score is 0.432, which is similar to the scores
achieved in similar tasks on molecular biology.
