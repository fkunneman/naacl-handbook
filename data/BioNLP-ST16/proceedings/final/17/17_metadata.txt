Abstract#==#For its fourth organization as a shared task, the Genia event extraction (GE)
task is refactored toward a general platform for shared information extraction
(IE) tasks, and for a IE-driven knowledge base (KB) system. On the newly
implemented shared task system, the GE task is run as an experimental task.
Final submissions were made by two systems, through which the functionality of
the system is thoroughly tested. The paper presents the new shared task system
and discusses on the final submissions.
