Abstract#==#In this paper we describe SoMaJo, a rule-based tokenizer for German web and
social media texts that was the best-performing system in the EmpiriST 2015
shared task with an average $F_1$-score of 99.57. We give an overview of the
system and the phenomena its rules cover, as well as a detailed error analysis.
The tokenizer is available as free software.
