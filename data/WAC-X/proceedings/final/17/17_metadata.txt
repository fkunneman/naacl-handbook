Abstract#==#This paper describes the construction of three corpora, intended for use in
social science research, comprising English-language, French-language and
Norwegian-language blogs related to the topic of climate change. The approach,
techniques and lessons learnt should be applicable for creating other
topically-focused blog corpora.
