Abstract#==#In this paper we present a framework for annotating the discourse and dialogue
structure of SMS message conversations. The annotation specifications integrate
elements of coherence-based discourse relations and communicative acts in
conversational speech. We present annotation experiments that show reliable
annotation can be achieved with this annotation framework.
