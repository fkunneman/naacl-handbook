Abstract#==#We release the Simple Paraphrase Database, a subset of of the Paraphrase
Database (PPDB) adapted for the task of text simplification. We train a
supervised model to associate simplification scores with each phrase pair,
producing rankings competitive with state-of-the-art lexical simplification
models. Our new simplification database contains 4.5 million paraphrase rules,
making it the largest available resource for lexical simplification.
