Abstract#==#We present a flexible method that re-arranges the ranked output of compound
splitters (i.e., decomposers of one-word compounds such as the German
Kinderlied ‘children’s song’) using a distributional semantics model. In
an experiment, we show that our re-ranker improves the quality of various
compound splitters.
